# Marketing Automation Assistant

Marketing Automation Assistant is an early-stage project focused on automating repetitive marketing workflows for paid media, ecommerce, and digital performance teams.

The goal is to help marketers, analysts, and account managers reduce manual work by transforming campaign data into clear insights, structured reports, optimization recommendations, and reusable workflows.

## Project Goals

- Automate paid media reporting workflows.
- Generate campaign insights from performance data.
- Support analysis for platforms such as Meta Ads, Google Ads, GA4, Shopify, and VTEX.
- Create reusable templates for marketing reports.
- Help teams identify performance changes, risks, and optimization opportunities faster.
- Reduce repetitive manual tasks in account management and campaign analysis.

## Problem

Marketing teams often spend a significant amount of time collecting data, reviewing metrics, preparing reports, and writing insights manually.

This creates operational workload and slows down decision-making, especially for teams managing several accounts, platforms, markets, and reporting cycles at the same time.

This project aims to bridge the gap between raw advertising data and practical marketing decisions.

## Initial Use Cases

### 1. Paid Media Reporting

Turn campaign exports from advertising platforms into structured summaries with key metrics, performance changes, and actionable insights.

### 2. Campaign Analysis

Identify changes in spend, ROAS, conversion rate, CTR, CPC, CPM, sessions, purchases, and revenue across different campaigns or time periods.

### 3. Insight Generation

Generate clear marketing insights using a consistent structure:

- Context
- Diagnosis
- Recommended action

### 4. Workflow Documentation

Document repeatable processes for account managers and paid media teams, including reporting, optimization, campaign reviews, and client communication.
